Community Partnership Initiative (CPI)

UNO values its reciprocal and mutually-beneficial relationship with its community partners. Above, you will find a small selection of those community partners.

The UNO CPI tool is an initiative of the UNO Community Engagement Measurement and Assessment Committee. This committee is dedicated to ongoing implementation of systematic and strategic community engagement data gathering, analysis, and reporting for UNO. This tool provides a way to collect and showcase community engagement projects and partnerships between our campus and community, while making community engagement data more accessible and consistent across campus.
